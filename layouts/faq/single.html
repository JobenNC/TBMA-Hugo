How much time should I allow for my makeup appointment?

If we are coming to you, extra time needs to be allotted for setting up and breaking down. It takes about 20-30 minutes to set everything up and 15-30 to break everything down.

It's important that someone is present while we pack up gear until we make our final exit.

The makeup appointment itself takes between 40-90 minutes. As a general rule of thumb we like to allow an hour per person and then add an hour for buffer time. We know that on event days there are interruptions and things that happen that cause delays so we want to ensure there is plenty of time for pampering!

I'm a bride, how much time should I allow for myself and my wedding party to get ready?

For weddings, We always allow 1 hour per person plus 1 hour for buffer at least. So if you have 4 people, we will allow 5 hours of get ready time. Twenty to thirty minutes is also added to the beginning and end of that get ready time for setting up and breaking down our equipment and kits.

When should I plan to be ready by before my event?

Beauty services should conclude at least 1-2 hours before your event. This will give you plenty of time to use the restroom, get into your attire, and take pictures before your ceremony (if it's before a wedding).
